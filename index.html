ABOUT US

Grah Sajja is an upholstery fabric retail shop which sells curtains, sofa fabric, sofa covers, bed sheets, bed linen, quilts, duets, roman curtain, roller blind, zebra blind etc. We also sell customized mattresses and sofas. We provide home decor services for curtains, sofas and other products including installations and customized services as well.
